About me

I'm a deep learning scientist with expertise in self-supervised and low resource machine learning in speech, language and image processing.

My early research was on keyword search/keyword spotting applications on low resource languages. I'm currently working on self-supervised deep learning applications for MRI cancer reserch, which involves super-resolution and physics-informed autoencoders.

Overview of my work

  • Open Vocabulary Keyword Search

    Acoustic unit discovery and contrastive learning for robust keyword detection.

  • Large Language Models

    Medical Language Processing using LLMs

  • MRI Super-resolution

    Self-supervised super-resolution with Implicit Neural Representations.

  • Physic-Informed Autonecoders

    Non-invasive Tissue Profiling from MRI
